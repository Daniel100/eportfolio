WHY I AM STUDING AGAIN?

I am keen to develop my skills and get updated about the newest advancements in Data Science. Besides this, I am eager to learn new content and develop my current skill set by facing new challenges. The online course from the University of Essex Online gives me an excellent opportunity to learn Data Science while simultaneously managing my career.

PROFESSIONAL CAREER

My whole career is dominated by data and how to gather information and knowledge from it, from modelling and analysing data to managing digital solutions. A large part of my work involves defining data-generating processes for use in business intelligence. I worked in a wide range of industries (automotive, fashion, and lifting solutions) and possess a wealth of experience in this field. Currently, I'm most interested in time series, and their forecasts mainly applied to sales figures. I studied statistics and hold a master's degree, which has helped give me the background to understand statistical and mathematical concepts well. Additionally, I have developed an excellent practical understanding of basic machine learning, basic database knowledge and SQL.
